How to use the Pomodoro Timer

The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method that uses a timer to break down work into intervals, typically 25 minutes in length, separated by short breaks.

  1. **Work Session:** Focus on a single task for **25 minutes**.
  2. **Short Break:** Take a **5-minute** break after each work session.
  3. **Long Break:** After four work sessions, take a longer break of **15-30 minutes**.

Use the buttons below to control the timer:

  • **Start:** Begins the work session timer.
  • **Pause:** Temporarily stops the timer.
  • **Reset:** Resets the timer to the beginning of the current session.
